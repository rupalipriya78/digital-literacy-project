##### **Stay Safe Online – Checklist** 



1. **Strong and Unique Passwords**
Make passwords strong and unique. Ensure passwords contain a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols. Also, don’t share the same password for multiple accounts.

2. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
Ensure two-factor authentication is enabled for email, social media, and banking applications.

3. **Avoid Clicking Suspicious Links**
Avoid clicking on suspicious links received through emails, messages, and pop-ups.

4. **Ensure Website Authenticity**
Ensure websites are authentic and contain https://, and then fill in the required details.

5. **Avoid Sharing Personal Information**
Do not share personal information like OTPs, passwords, etc., with anyone.

6. **Public Wi-Fi Safety**

&#x20;   Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking applications and transactions.



**UPI / Financial Safety** 



1. **Never Approve Unknown Transactions**
Do not approve any UPI collect requests from unknown senders. Ensure the sender is authentic.

2. **UPI ID Verification**
Verify the UPI ID while sending money to anyone to avoid fraud.

3. **Do Not Share OTP and Bank Credentials**
Banks will not ask for OTPs and passwords over calls and messages. Do not share them with anyone.
