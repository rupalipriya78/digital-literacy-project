#### **Social Media Do’s and Don’ts**



|Do|Don'ts|
|-|-|
|**1.Maintain a professional profile**|1.**Don’t share personal or sensitive information**|
|**2.Think before posting**|2.**Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content**|
|**3.Use privacy settings**|3.**Don’t spread fake news or rumors**|
|**4.Share useful and positive content**|4.**Don’t engage in online arguments or bullying**|
|**5.Respect others online**|5.**Don’t click or share suspicious links**|
